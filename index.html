Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

What Nudge does

Nudge is a private photo and message sharing app for small groups of friends and family. Only people you invite — or who invite you — can see your posts.

Data we collect

  • Email address (used to create and identify your account)
  • Display name
  • Photos you choose to share
  • Text messages you write in response to daily prompts
  • Device push-notification token (to deliver notifications)

How your data is used

Your photos and messages are shared exclusively with members of your group. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

Where data is stored

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage), hosted in the EU. By using Nudge you agree to Google's terms of service at firebase.google.com.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete account. Deleting your account removes your profile and sign-in credentials. Photos and messages you posted remain visible to other group members as part of the shared history.

Children

Nudge is not directed at children under 13.
